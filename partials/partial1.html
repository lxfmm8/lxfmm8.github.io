Welcome to A Whiff of Design. This site is created by Tim Xiaofeng Lin [resume], an interaction designer, information architect, and toy maker. He has a BFA in visual communication design from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, and he is currently working on his MFA at Parsons the New School for Design, Design and Technology program. He embraces all forms of art and design and try to re-construct them in various ways. He strongly believes in function, concept, and social meaning.

Welcome to A Whiff of Design by Xiaofeng Lin, who is an New York City based interaction designer, illustrator, writer, and mixed-media artist.

I believe that the emerging of different media and technology shall be based on the purpose of reinforcing the delivery process of information; otherwise it will bring out the state of confusion along with the loss of pure and essential experience of enjoyment. Secondly, I have an absolute belief in craft. Craftsmanship of design reflects our social and moral responsibility of a designer.

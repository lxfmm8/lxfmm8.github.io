Welcome to A Whiff of Design. This site is created and maintained by Tim Xiaofeng Lin, an experience designer, writer, and artist. He has a BFA in visual communication design from the School of the Art Institute of Chicago, and he is currently working on his MFA at Parsons the New School for Design, Design and Technology program. He embraces all forms of art and design and try to re-construct them in various ways. He strongly believes in function, structure, concept, and social meaning. His current focuses are design-based web development and children's toys. Click on thumbnail images or the menu link to start browsing. Thank you.

Yuhua Agate

Digital Branding of A Web Application
for Agate Pickers & Collectors

Design & Development Individual Project
My Role UI, Branding, Photography & Retouching
Tools Photoshop, Lightroom, Illustrator, HTML, CSS, JS, Bootstrap

Design Goal

This is a layout / visual concept for Yuhua stone website. The site may consist of sections such as introduction about the stones, history, geographical locations, how to discern the stones, and an online store. The theme of the site was created around the thought that “Natural colors are never solid”. Gaussian blur is used to turn textures of the agates into bakcgrounds of the interface. Besides color branding, the project aims to demonsatrate a new potential look and feel of a combination of information / analysis / story / e-commerce multi-platform website of a natural product.

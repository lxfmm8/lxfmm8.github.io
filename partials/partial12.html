Chain of Thought

Vector Illustration
Skills / Tools: Illustrator
Advisor: Self-Directed


My first personal illustration work as an interaction designer. I aim to create a series of imageries that are dynamic and interrelated using clean lines and shapes to convey ideas. Letters are used as perspective transformers. By showing my chain of thought, I try to inspire, not to impress the audience.

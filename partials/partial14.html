Stats Visual System

User Interface

Director: Self-Directed
Tools: Illustrator, GIMP, Framer
Year: 2016

The project was created around one single idea of mine - linear representation being applied to stats report system of basketball (and potentially other sports). In order for the symbols of actions to work in different types of information architecture, they have to be bold, minimal, and representitive, and the symbols should add a little excitement and visual interest to the originally plain and boring tables of numbers.

Stats Visual System

Visual Components for Modern Sports Literal Live Broadcasting

My Role: Visual concept, UI, UX
Tools: Illustrator, GIMP
Year: 2016-2017

Design Purpose

Literal Sports Live Broadcasting has long been a useful method for people who want to watch the games on the move, but it has transitioned to video broadcasting in the recent years. However, there still may be space fot simple text-based components on the screens. Is there a way to make these texts more lively and interesting?

User Research

Most game watchers won't actually pay for the sports programs. Many sports fans either watch the games on TV at home or in the bars, or they go online to find a free live-streaming website, which, most of the time, is both full of ads and of bad image quality and streaming-speed.

Personas / Use Cases

Tom / 23 years / College Student / Basketball Fan


“I'm very busy with my school work, so I can't catch up with all the games I want to watch. I sometimes have to constantly refresh my phone to get the latest scores, when I'm in class. The pure text broadcasting is very boring.”

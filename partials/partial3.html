Project Brief

This collection of packaging projects demonstrates custom templates that are folded and glued from one piece of paper. Using Silhouette Studio, I created modified versions of templates, and I made custom fonts for the new branding of products (tea leaves, gummy bears, font cards, beads, and fortune cookies)that are conventionally not packaged in these ways / styles. This series is featured in Behance Graphic Design Gallery.
